About Me

Hello there! I'm Nathan Hawkes - a designer, developer, and creative technologist who loves building thoughtful, user-focused digital experiences. I specialise in developing websites with WordPress, but I also enjoy crafting custom PHP and HTML-based projects, such as Colour Library or Who Broke The News?. I work fluidly across design and code, bringing ideas to life through clean interfaces, responsive layouts, and purposeful functionality.

I've been working as a developer in various capacities since 2007, and I became fully self-employed in 2016, creating websites for small businesses, nonprofits, and new startups. With a background in education, I've delivered workshops on everything from Basic Computing to Managing Your WordPress Website. I also have nearly two decades of experience in the nonprofit sector, where I've helped organisations communicate better through well-designed digital tools and platforms.

You can see some of the work I've done over the years by visiting my portfolio at nathanhawkes.dev. You can read some of my blog posts, find out how to get in touch, or even help support some of these projects. Perhaps you might consider buyng me a coffee? Just a thought ...

Outside of development work, I love writing fiction and occasionally lose myself in the realms of science fiction and fantasy. I'm especially drawn to projects that explore new worlds - whether that's through storytelling, speculative technology, or innovative design. I have a passion for gameplay, especially tabletop role playing games such as Dungeons and Dragons (although I have yet to actually play a game, so hit me up if you want to add a newbie to your group).

If you'd like to chat, collaborate, or learn more about what I do, feel free to drop me an email at nathan@nathanhawkes.dev. It'd be great to hear from you.
